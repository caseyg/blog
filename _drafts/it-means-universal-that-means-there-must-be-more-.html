Lobe Den Herren - Praise To The Lord

meeting Sr. Necitas; tschokolade - liturgy of the hours in the dom, urologist dawn light filtering

and then again in our living room

and again, chanting, in mundelein

that the same god whose people chanted in Nyanja, wrapped in chitenge printed with bold blue mary

is the same god whose good-humored priests and people enfolded me in lectio in ohio snowstorms

and the same god whose aging priest ministered with great devotion to a heartbreakingly empty, tiny church in the middle of manhattan where little italy and chinatown meet -- a man tenderly honoring his bride

is the same god who stands, hard to hear, amidst the electric fans and powerpoints with psalm responses in the philippines -- and in the rolling meadows in tanay (the only place in the Philippines I've actually felt close to God)

the same god sitting quietly in missouri as i cried after confession in the midst of dancing, who sent two seminarians to pray with me the next day after eucharistic service

who's up already by the time i stagger (on occasion) to st. boniface at 6:30am on weekdays to receive him

the same god
