I occasionally put class notes on my blog when I want to rethink/revisit/remember things we've done.

Today's Fundamentals (dance) class was an introduction to basic principles of the Alexander Technique. I don't remember all of them, but the idea is that gentle thinking about these directions -- rather than muscular forcing -- can help open up and align your body properly.

  1. Hips back (and over ankles)
  2. Head forward (and up)
  3. Shoulders wide
  4. Knees forward
  5. Elbows back
  6. Neck free
